About Mathura

Mathura, the birthplace of Lord Krishna, is one of the most sacred cities in India. It’s filled with ancient temples, beautiful ghats, and divine energy.

Famous Places

Visit the Vishram Ghat, Dwarkadhish Temple, and Krishna Janmabhoomi for spiritual experiences. The Yamuna river adds unmatched beauty to the city.

Culture & Festivals

Mathura is known for its vibrant Holi celebration, traditional sweets, and spiritual gatherings that attract visitors from across the world.
